My Best Mediterranean cruise trip

On our mediterranean cruise trip that started from Barcelona, I and my family members (my husband and 2 daughters) visited some of the most coveted destinations in Europe, including seaside villages in Italy, culinary capitals in Spain, scenic ports in the French Rivera, and historic cities in Portugal and Croatia. Also we visited Paris before embarking our cruise from Barcelona. We reached Barcelona 1 day before so also got chance to stroll through the broad boulevards of Barcelona.

The places we visited:

  • Paris, France
  • Barcelona, Spain
  • Ajaccio, Spain
  • Marseille, France
  • Cannes, France
  • Nice, France
  • Genoa, Italy
  • Pisa, Italy
  • Livorno, Italy
  • Florence, Italy
  • Rome, Italy

Paris

We booked our flight from Washington DC (IAD) to Paris, France(CDG). When we landed in Paris, since it is around 5 hours ealier time zone, it was still evening but during that time in August, it was windy day in Paris. We booked a cab from airport and went to our Hotel. In the evening, we visited the famous church, Montmartre. The next day, we went to Eiffel Tower. We also took boat tour to other places in Paris. The Bachir icecream at Montmartre outlet is extremely popular and definately one of the best icecream parlours in Paris.

Barcelona

We flew from Paris to Barcelona early morning. It was my little ones birthday and she was so excited to celebrate her birthday. From airport, we took a cab and went to our hotel. We took rest for a few hours and started our stroll in Barcelona. The weather was perfect that day. We found a perfect vegan cafe to start our day with. Then we head to Sagrada_Familia

The real fun starts now: Our most awaited cruise trip!

We boarded the cruise from Barcelona port at 1 pm. After passing through the security, finally we arrived in our cruise and our room which had balcony.
